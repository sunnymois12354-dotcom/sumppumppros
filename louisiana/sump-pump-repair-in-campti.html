Signs You Need a Sump Pump Repair Pro in Campti

Catch these early in Campti's humid climate.

01

Strange noises from the sump pump

Banging, clattering, or hissing sounds can indicate serious mechanical issues.

02

Frequent cycles

If your pump runs continuously or frequently cycles on and off, it may be struggling to keep up.

03

Water in the basement or crawl space

Visible water accumulation can indicate failure or inefficiencies in the sump pump.

04

Power outages during storms

If your sump pump doesn't have a battery backup, it can fail during power outages.

05

Corrosion or rust on the pump

Visible corrosion can indicate wear and might lead to pump failure if not addressed.

Increased humidity levels
