Seasonal Sump Pump Repair Tips for Crumpton

How Crumpton's humid subtropical climate changes sump pump needs through the year.

Spring

Spring brings heavy rainfall, which can increase the risk of flooding.

Tip: Ensure your sump pump is functioning correctly before storms hit.

Summer

Hot temperatures can lead to increased humidity, affecting your sump pump's performance.

Tip: Consider regular maintenance checks to avoid overheating.

Fall

Leaves and debris can clog drainage systems as they fall.

Tip: Schedule a pre-winter inspection to clear any blockages.

Winter

Frozen discharge lines can cause sump pump failures during cold snaps.

Tip: Insulate pipes and check your pump regularly to ensure it's ready.
